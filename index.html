Havat HaChaim — the Farm of Life

Thirty three dunams on a hill in the southern Golan, within sight of the Sea of Galilee. A place of hospitality, retreat, and rest, built by hand through hard years of war and emptied trails. The mountain is still standing, and so is the work.

A House of Healing Since October 7

The same hill that welcomes pilgrims now also shelters the broken.

From the day of October 7, even through our own hardest season, the farm gave itself to a calling larger than tourism. We open our gates to the survivors of the Nova festival and to soldiers, families, and wounded souls across Israel who carry the trauma of this war. We hold gatherings of healing here, where the quiet of the Golan, the open sky, and the presence of others who understand begin to mend what the violence tore. That is the truest thing this land does. It heals.
