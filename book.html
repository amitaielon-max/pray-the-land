A book written on a wall

For months, letters went out from a farm on a hill in the southern Golan Heights to a wall of watchmen scattered across the world. A cliff where Jesus asked His question at the enemy's front door. A roof in Capernaum torn open by four friends. A river of twelve stones. A tomb whose whole treasure is an absence. A harbor where the gospel boarded a ship and went out looking for you.

Now the letters are a book. It was written by a guide, a farmer, and a father, and it belongs to everyone who prayed it into being.

"I have posted watchmen on your walls, O Jerusalem. They will never be silent, day or night."

ISAIAH 62:6
